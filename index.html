A Discussion on Differential Expression and Co-Expression Network Analysis

Explore the world of data science and machine learning in the context of analyzing gene expression data. This discussion delves into the techniques and methodologies for conducting differential expression analysis and constructing co-expression networks to unravel the biological pathways of gene regulation associated with specific diseases. Discover how statistics and data science play a pivotal role in understanding the intricate molecular mechanisms underlying various conditions.

Topics include:

  • Differential expression analysis of gene expression data
  • Co-expression network construction
  • Identification of biological pathways related to gene regulation
  • Utilization of statistics and data science in disease research
